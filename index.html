A Voice That Transcends Generations

Kattassery Joseph Yesudas, popularly known as Dr. K. J. Yesudas, is an Indian playback singer and musician who sings predominantly in Malayalam, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, and Hindi films. Often referred to as Gaanagandharvan (The Celestial Singer), he is regarded as one of the greatest singers in the history of Indian music.

With a career spanning over six decades, Yesudas has recorded over 40,000 songs in various Indian languages, including devotional, classical, and film music. He has won numerous accolades, including eight National Film Awards, five Filmfare Awards South, and the Padma Shri, Padma Bhushan, and Padma Vibhushan from the Government of India.

His mellifluous voice and emotive singing have made him a legend across South India and beyond, with fans spanning multiple generations and continents.
