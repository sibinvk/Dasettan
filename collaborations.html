Ilaiyaraaja

5000+ Songs

1977 - Present

The most prolific and celebrated collaboration in Indian cinema history. Together they created thousands of timeless melodies across Tamil, Malayalam, Telugu, and Kannada.

Iconic Songs:

  • "Manjil Virinja Pookkal" - Malayalam classic
  • "Nizhalgal Ravi" - Tamil masterpiece
  • "Vedam Anuvanuvuna" - National Award winner (Telugu)
  • "En Kanmani" - National Award winner (Tamil)
  • "Andhi Mazhai Megam" - Philosophical gem
Together Achieved: 4 National Film Awards, 10+ Filmfare Awards, Revolutionized South Indian film music

G. Devarajan

2000+ Songs

1962 - 1980

The maestro who dominated Malayalam cinema in the 1960s-70s. Their partnership defined the golden era of Malayalam film music.

Notable Works:

  • "Omana Thingal Kidavo" - First National Award
  • "Padunna Puzha" - Evergreen classic
  • "Azhakil" songs series

M.S. Baburaj

1500+ Songs

  • "Neelaambari" songs
  • "Hridayathin Nizhalil"
